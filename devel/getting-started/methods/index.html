    Methods

    We currently implement the following methods:

    Data valuation¶

    • LOO.

    • Permutation Shapley (also called ApproxShapley) (Castro et al., 2009)1.

    • TMCS (Ghorbani and Zou, 2019)2.

    • Data Banzhaf (Wang and Jia, 2023)3.

    • Beta Shapley (Kwon and Zou, 2022)4.

    • CS-Shapley (Schoch et al., 2022)5.

    • Least Core (Yan and Procaccia, 2021)6.

    • Owen Sampling (Okhrati and Lipani, 2021)7.

    • Data Utility Learning (Wang et al., 2022)8.

    • kNN-Shapley (Jia et al., 2019)9.

    • Group Testing (Jia et al., 2019)10

    • Data-OOB (Kwon and Zou, 2023)11.

    Influence functions¶

    • CG Influence. (Koh and Liang, 2017)12.

    • Direct Influence (Koh and Liang, 2017)12.

    • LiSSA (Agarwal et al., 2017)13.

    • Arnoldi Influence (Schioppa et al., 2022)14.

    • EKFAC Influence (George et al., 2018; Martens and Grosse, 2015)1516.

    • Nyström Influence, based on the ideas in (Hataya and Yamada, 2023)17 for bi-level optimization.
